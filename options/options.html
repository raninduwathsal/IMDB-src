Get your own OMDb API key

  1. Visit omdbapi.com/apikey.aspx.
  2. Select the Free plan and submit the form with your email.
  3. Check your email to receive and activate your key.
  4. Return here, enter your key above, then click Save.

The built‑in shared key can hit its daily limit and cause searches to fail. Adding your own key ensures uninterrupted use.
